## The Recipe

> **Ingredients:**
>
> - A community of passionate Chef cookbook maintainers
> - Cookbooks in need of a loving home
> - A generous helping of open source spirit
>
> **Method:** Adopt unloved cookbooks. Maintain with care. Improve over time. Serve warm to the community. 🥘

We adopt cookbooks that are looking for a new home — whether the maintainer has moved on
from Chef or simply no longer wants the burden of maintaining it alone. No cookbook left behind.
